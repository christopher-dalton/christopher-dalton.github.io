The Hacker Chronicles

Tyler, a small town computer whiz, is recruited into the top computer science programs in the country by an ex-Silicon Valley hotshot turned professor. He is quickly seduced by money and the perks, for winning a hack-a-thon and proving himself as one of the “Elite”. In doing so, he becomes totally oblivious to the more sinister side of the university. Once he realizes that the university is a front for a team of international corporate hackers, he tries to leave. However, leaving is not an option and Tyler has to move quickly and utilize every resource he can to escape and —uncover— Hacker U.
